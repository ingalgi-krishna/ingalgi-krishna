## About

I am **Krishna Ingalgi**, Founder and Chief Operating Officer of **Sucetas Technologies**, a custom software and AI development company I co-founded in September 2024 and bootstrapped to production deployments without outside capital.

I run operations for the services business in Pune and product for our SaaS line under the UK entity. The part I refuse to give up is the building. I have personally shipped our e-commerce platform, all three company websites, ongoing Dunefox product features, and our AI voice agent system. Most weeks I am in the roadmap and in the codebase on the same day, and I would not know how to do this job otherwise.

What I care about is applied AI that survives contact with real users: retrieval-augmented conversational agents, real-time voice, and the product judgement to know which problems are worth solving. If I cannot build it, I should not be promising it to a customer.

> Good technology should feel invisible. It just works, and makes life a little bit better.

**Focus:** AI product development, conversational and voice AI, SaaS architecture, full-stack engineering (Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL), cloud (AWS, GCP), startup operations and go-to-market.

## What Sucetas delivers

Beyond the products I build, our services team ships computer vision and AI systems for clients under NDA. **Production intelligence** for injection molding plants runs on one IP camera per machine with no machine modification, hitting better than 98% accuracy where sensor-based approaches cap below 95%. **Vision-based HRMS** replaces cards and fingerprints with face-recognition attendance and payroll, offline-first and on-premise. Credit for those belongs to our services engineering team.
